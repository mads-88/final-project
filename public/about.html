Our Story

A small, family-owned donut shop that quietly became the unofficial “meet you there?” spot for downtown workers and college students.

Downtown Donuts has been rolling dough and brewing coffee since 1992. Long before food delivery apps and smartphones, this shop was the place you stopped before work, after school, or on your way to a game.

These days, you’ll still see regulars at the counter — plus students with laptops, friends splitting a dozen, and parents bribing kids with sprinkle rings. The goal hasn’t changed: warm donuts, kind faces, and a place that feels like part of your routine.
